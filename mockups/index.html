v0.16 — Movement Range Enforcement
Movement range enforcement prototype. Select a unit, click NORMAL MOVE or ADVANCE to enter move mode. Ghost circles show start positions, shaded zones show maximum movement boundary. Dragging beyond M" snaps to boundary. CONFIRM commits the move; CANCEL snaps back.
